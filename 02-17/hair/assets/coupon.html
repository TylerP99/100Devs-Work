Welcome to Fabrizio Salon & Urban Retreat Spa

Your beauty is our passion.

Located in the charming Trolley Square area of Wilmington, Delaware since 1993, our award-winning salon offers the European craft of hairdressing to women, men, and children. Nestled on the 2nd floor of our modern, rustic, completely renovated 1890’s era townhouse, you’ll find our Urban Retreat Spa where we offer top-rated face, skin, waxing, massage and nail services without the hassle of an overnight stay, or the expensive price tag.

We carry professional brand products including Arrojo Hair Care, Bumble & bumble, Wella, Neuma Organic Hair Care, Rhonda Allison Skin Care, Dermalogica Skin Care, Nioxin, and our very own Fabrizio Hair Care brand imported exclusively from the hills of Tuscany.

We specialize in long-lasting, silky, smooth blowouts, balayage highlights, professional hair color, repairing hair treatments, hair smoothing systems, bridal updos, hair extensions, facials, peels and microdermabrasion.
